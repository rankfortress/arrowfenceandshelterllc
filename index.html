Arrow Fence & Shelter LLC

Arrow Fence & Shelter is a small, local and family owned company. Our owner began his fence career in the fence wholesale industry at the early age of 20 years old. After several years of dedicate service in sales and management we took the leap into small business ownership in 2003. Now here we are 17 years later and Arrow Fence & Shelter has become one of the most reliable fence companies in Oklahoma When it comes to getting the job done right, Arrow Fence & Shelter knows how to deliver. Our finished product does the speaking for us and we gladly stand behind all of our work and products with a 1 year workmanship warranty. We currently serve Central Oklahoma in residential fencing and travel up to 1,000 miles for certain commercial and government fence projects. We have recently completed or are in the process of completing projects in Arkansas, Colorado, Kansas, Missouri, Texas, and of course Oklahoma.
